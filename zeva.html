Zeva.ng // Frictionless Event Ticketing Engine

A fast ticketing system. It splits payments automatically, pays organizers the next day, and lets them scan QR codes to check in guests at the door.

The Problem

Most ticketing websites force customers to create accounts, fill out long forms, and wait days for payouts. Organizers face complex setups and delayed access to ticket sales money.

The Solution

Zeva provides a 10-second checkout needing only a name and email. Ticket sales split automatically at purchase, routing money directly to organizers next-day with capped fees.

Workflow & Split Calculations

Instead of paying event organizers manually, the system calculates and splits ticket money at checkout. The host's share is routed directly to their bank account next-day, and platform commissions transfer automatically, keeping payments simple and safe.

3. Unique Key Points

  • Capped Platform Fees: platform commission is capped at ₦5,000 maximum per ticket on luxury passes, offering massive savings for high-ticket events.
  • No Escrow Risks: platform never holds client funds over weekends; settlements happen directly next-day.
  • Setup Fee: Free events pay a flat ₦5,000 setup fee to filter out spammers.
  • Accreditation: Automated gate checking with QR-code scanning via smartphone camera.
