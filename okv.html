OKV // Osumare KreatiVision CSR Placements

A system for event branding, ticketing, and seat bookings designed to coordinate sponsors and VIP guests during large cultural events.

The Problem

Cultural events and theaters face scheduling conflicts when assigning seat numbers, managing sponsor branding zones, and distributing VIP access invitations.

The Solution

OKV implements a seating coordinator portal, enabling sponsors to claim guest tickets directly and aligning seat layouts with ticket categories.

Plato's Psychosis Partnership

Collaborating with the Ogun State Ministry of Culture and Tourism to organize the "Plato's Psychosis" arts renaissance at the June 12 Cultural Centre in Abeokuta, this system automated sponsor registration for Special Recognition Awards.

3. Seating Accreditation Pipeline

The system manages seating charts and lists of award winners to automatically distribute free VIP tickets. It also tracks sponsor contributions, making it simple for organizers to handle corporate tickets.
