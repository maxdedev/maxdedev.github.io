Developer Story

"I'm the kind of person who isn't afraid of challenges. Challenges are opportunities for growth, and I thrive in environments that push me to think creatively and solve complex problems."

My journey started in graphic design, where I learned how to make things look beautiful and clean. Soon, I wanted to build tools that actually work behind the scenes. I taught myself coding and focused on building secure databases, protecting websites, and handling payments.

Whether I am checking a website for security problems, fixing bugs that make systems crash, or designing mobile app screens, I always focus on finding simple solutions. I believe challenges are just opportunities to create something better.
