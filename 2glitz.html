2Glitz // Subscription Real Estate Marketplace

A subscription website connecting home seekers with trusted agents. It automatically adds watermarks to property photos and protects agent details.

The Problem

Real estate boards are cluttered with spam listings, duplicated photos, and unverified agents, leaving property searchers vulnerable to scams and fake listings.

The Solution

2Glitz filters agents through a verification tier. It restricts listing access with gated details, requiring users to log in before obtaining contact cards.

Gated Contact Leads

Property searchers must log in to view an agent's contact details or send messages. This prevents automated spam harvesting, tracks search interests, and provides agents with direct, verifiable leads.

3. Media Watermarking & Compression

To stop other people from stealing listing photos, the site automatically adds a 2Glitz watermark logo on all uploaded pictures. It also automatically shrinks video files to save server space and reduce hosting bills.
